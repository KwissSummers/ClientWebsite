About Me

I'm Ohm Desai, the indie developer behind Sock Games. I love building games that are intimate, quirky, and emotionally resonant. My goal is to create thoughtful digital spaces—small games with big feelings.

What inspired your work?

Games like EarthBound, Outer Wilds, and small RPGMaker stories—anything heartfelt and offbeat.

Favorite games?

Celeste, Hollow Knight, Undertale, and anything with a tight loop and strong personality.

Your creative process?

Prototype → Theme → Polish. It usually starts with a mechanic and grows into something personal.
